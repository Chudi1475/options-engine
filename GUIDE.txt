================================================
  OPTIONS ALERT BOT — HOW TO READ AND USE IT
================================================

WHAT THIS IS
------------
A Telegram bot that watches the market every morning (9:45-10:30 AM ET)
and texts you when a high-probability options setup appears. It is built
from a real trader's order history — it looks for the exact pattern
behind his best trades and ignores everything else. After an alert, it
keeps watching YOUR trade all day and texts you every exit step.

The bot NEVER trades for you. It texts a suggestion. You decide.
Every message ends "Your call." because it genuinely is.

THE MORNING MESSAGE (risk mode for the day)
-------------------------------------------
🟢 RISK MODE: GREEN  = normal day, standard rules
🟡 RISK MODE: YELLOW = something's off (Fed speech, high fear, big gap).
                       Alerts still come with a warning banner. Smaller size.
🔴 RISK MODE: RED    = major event day (FOMC, CPI, PPI, jobs report) or
                       panic. Consider sitting out. Any alert is HALF size.

THE ENTRY ALERT (this is the money text)
-----------------------------------------
  💰 EXPECTED: +10.5% per trade after est. costs
  (NEW-RULES BACKTEST, approx pricing — live stats take over after 30 signals)

  📈 BUY CALL — SPXW 7300, expires TODAY 6/11
  Quote: Bid $4.20 / Ask $4.60 — set a LIMIT order near $4.40. Never market-order.
  Size: ~3.3% of account (≈ $830) — risking 1% if stopped

  WHY: SPX just turned UP in the last 15 minutes (+0.21%) — the exact
  pattern behind Kelechi's best trades.

  EXIT PLAN — I'll text you each step:
  1️⃣ SELL HALF at +25%
  2️⃣ momentum flips → I text you → sell the rest
  3️⃣ STOP: -30% → sell everything
  4️⃣ expires today → I warn you 15 min before close

  🔴 Win rate in testing: 57 of 100 — RISKY (...)
  Your call.

WHAT EACH LINE MEANS
--------------------
EXPECTED — the average % this exact setup made per trade in testing,
  after estimated costs. This is the number that matters. (Win rate alone
  can lie: a setup can win often and still lose money.)

Quote / LIMIT — buy with a LIMIT order near the middle price shown.
  NEVER use a market order on options; the spread will eat you.
  If the price already ran way past the quote, skip the trade — chasing
  was not what got backtested.

Size — how much to put in so that a full stop-out costs you exactly 1% of
  your account. Text /setaccount 25000 to the bot once and it shows real
  dollar amounts. "CORRELATED — half size" means you already have a
  same-direction trade open, so this one is smaller.

Win rate — small line on purpose: 57 of 100 sounds worse than 72 of 100,
  but the new exit rules make MORE money per trade by letting winners run.
  Expected value is the honest headline.

THE EXIT TEXTS (the bot watches your trade so you don't have to)
----------------------------------------------------------------
💰 SELL HALF — +25% target hit........sell half, let the rest ride
   (only bought 1 contract? just sell it — banking the win is the play)
🔄 MOMENTUM FLIPPED — SELL REMAINING..the move died, sell what's left
🛑 STOP — SELL EVERYTHING.............down 30%. Sell. No hoping.
⏰ CLOSE BEFORE EXPIRY................15 min to the bell, close it out

Option prices in these texts come from a live quote when available
(may be ~15 min delayed) or a clearly-labeled estimate — your broker
shows the real number, the instruction is what matters.

HOW TO TAKE THE TRADE ON ROBINHOOD (60 seconds)
------------------------------------------------
1. Open Robinhood, search the ticker from the text
   (SPXW alerts = search "SPX". Robinhood shows SPX index options.)
2. Tap "Trade" -> "Trade Options"
3. Pick the EXPIRATION DATE from the text (top of screen)
4. Pick CALL or PUT (matches the text)
5. Find the STRIKE from the text (e.g. 7300)
6. Tap it -> "Buy" -> set a LIMIT order at the price from the text
   -> enough contracts to fit the Size line -> submit.
   If it doesn't fill in a minute or two, you may bump the limit a few
   cents toward the Ask. If the option already ran far past the quoted
   price, let it go. There's another trade tomorrow.
7. Then just follow the bot's texts: sell half at +25%, sell the rest on
   the MOMENTUM FLIPPED text, and if the STOP text comes — sell. Don't
   hope. Don't average down. Sell.

TEXTING THE BOT (commands)
--------------------------
/setaccount 25000  -> tells it your account size (sizes cards in dollars)
/status            -> risk mode, account, open positions right now
/risk red          -> override today's risk mode by hand
/test              -> sends fake examples of all 5 alert types
/help              -> the list above

THE THREE RULES THAT KEEP YOU ALIVE
------------------------------------
1. If you didn't get a text, there is no trade. Boredom is not a setup.
2. The stop is the stop. One ignored stop can erase a week of wins.
3. Size off the text (~3.3% of account). Losing streaks happen even when
   nothing is wrong — size so that 5 losses in a row barely sting.

HONESTY NOTES (read once)
-------------------------
- Stats come from a real backtest over ~3 months of market data using
  approximated option pricing, and the bot says exactly which rules were
  backtested on every card. After 30 real tracked signals, the cards
  switch to live results automatically.
- Every Friday after the close it sends a scoreboard: what the new exit
  rules actually did vs the old rules vs what the backtest claimed.
- Past performance does not guarantee future results.
- This is not financial advice. Your call.
