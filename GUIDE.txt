================================================
  OPTIONS ALERT BOT — HOW TO READ AND USE IT
================================================

WHAT THIS IS
------------
A Telegram bot that watches the market every morning (9:45-10:30 AM ET)
and texts you when a high-probability options setup appears. It is built
from a real trader's order history — it looks for the exact pattern
behind his best trades and ignores everything else.

The bot NEVER trades for you. It texts a suggestion. You decide.

THE MORNING MESSAGE (comes first, around market open)
------------------------------------------------------
🟢 NORMAL DAY  = trade the alerts as normal
🟠 CAUTION DAY = something is off (high fear, big gap, Fed/CPI day).
                 Alerts still come, but consider HALF size.
🔴 NO-TRADE DAY = market is in panic mode. Bot stands down. Don't trade.

THE ALERT (this is the money text)
-----------------------------------
Example:

  🔴 WIN RATE: 72% — RISKY

  📈 BUY CALL — SPXW 7395
  Expires: 5/13
  Size: 10% of account

  WHY: SPX just turned UP over the last 15 minutes. Buying calls when
  momentum has just turned up is the exact pattern behind the best
  trades. In testing, this setup won 72 out of every 100 trades.

  EXIT PLAN: sell at +15% profit. Get out if it drops -60%.
  BACKTESTED: YES (...real numbers...)
  Your call.

WHAT EACH LINE MEANS
--------------------
WIN RATE line — how often this exact setup made money in testing:
  🔴 70-74%  RISKY        (wins 7 of 10, but rough ride)
  🟠 75-79%  DECENT ODDS
  🟢 80-84%  GOOD ODDS
  🟢🌟 85%+   GREAT ODDS
  Below 70%? You never see it. The bot stays silent instead of
  forcing a weak trade. No text = no trade today. That's normal.

BUY CALL / BUY PUT — the direction. 📈 CALL = betting it goes up.
  📉 PUT = betting it goes down.

The number after the ticker (7395) — the STRIKE price.

Expires — the expiration date to pick. Same day for SPX = "0DTE".

Size — never put more than 10% of your account into one trade.

HOW TO TAKE THE TRADE ON ROBINHOOD (60 seconds)
------------------------------------------------
1. Open Robinhood, search the ticker from the text
   (SPXW alerts = search "SPX". Robinhood shows SPX index options.)
2. Tap "Trade" -> "Trade Options"
3. Pick the EXPIRATION DATE from the text (top of screen)
4. Pick CALL or PUT (matches the text)
5. Find the STRIKE from the text (e.g. 7395)
6. Tap it -> "Buy" -> enter how many contracts fit inside 10% of
   your account -> swipe to submit at the ASK (or a nickel below)
7. IMMEDIATELY set your exit:
   - Set a LIMIT SELL at +15% above what you paid (the profit target)
   - Watch the position: if it drops 60% from what you paid, sell.
     Don't hope. Don't average down. Sell.
8. Everything closes the SAME DAY. Do not hold 0DTE past the close —
   it expires worthless at 4 PM if it's out of the money.

THE THREE RULES THAT KEEP YOU ALIVE
------------------------------------
1. If you didn't get a text, there is no trade. Boredom is not a setup.
2. The stop is the stop. One ignored stop can erase a week of wins.
3. 10% of account max. A 70% win rate still loses 3 times out of 10 —
   sometimes back to back. Size so that 3 losses in a row don't hurt.

HONESTY NOTES (read once)
-------------------------
- Win rates come from a real backtest over ~3 months of market data
  using approximated option pricing. They are estimates, not promises.
- Past performance does not guarantee future results.
- This is not financial advice. Every alert ends with "Your call."
  because it genuinely is.
